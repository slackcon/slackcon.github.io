What is SlackCON?

SlackCON is a community effort to organize an annual four-day event targeting the Slackware Linux distribution, the oldest Linux distribution still under development.

The event is a real training ground, it serve to bring the community closer, learn more about Slackware, new technologies and of course help in the development of the project:

  • Disclosure
  • Advertising
  • Ideas
  • Bug report
  • Documentation
  • Slackbuilds Maintainer
  • Commercial
  • Donation Forms for the Slackware Project
  • Packaging for Community
  • Technologies for the GNU/Linux World in General

This allows us to keep an even more faithful community and seed project knowledge to those who do not know it. So we can make the community stronger without any barriers.

The initial idea is to vary the states of Brazil annually, that is, every year the event is organized in a different state in Brazil and maybe later in the world.
The voting of cities is done by the community, anyone can vote.

Initially the event is not endorsed by the Slackware project, as said it is a community effort we are not also a company. Everything is done in a collaborative way by the community. NO PROFIT.
