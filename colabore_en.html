How Can I Collaborate?

The SLACKCON is a community service developed by the Slackware Community and WITH NO PROFIT.
There are ways you can contribute for the realization of this event:
  • Sponsoring this event:

    You can sponsor the event where will be registered your logo in our homepage banners, shirt stamps, slides and ads of the event. And this will remain for the event's history.

    All funds that we get will be repassed for the event, to be made in the city chosen by the community.
    To choose the city a votation will be made where everyone has a voice, no exceptions.

    But where the donations/funds will be applied???

    • Domain/Hosting: Pay the domain and hosting of this website yearly.
    • Giveaways: Shirts,certificates, stickers, name tag and bottons. For all the participants.
    • Banners: To spread in the event.
    • Meal: Dinner and afternoon lunch for the participants.
    • Localization: As we know in Brazil, Slackware is not supported by universities, being the first and organized event we have to designate an enviroment to organize the event.
      In the future who knows we'll get the support of the universities or even the hacklabs.
    • Help to the Project: One percent of the fund will be sent to the Slackware project. This will help the creator of the project o Patrick Volkerding, without the effort of the creator on the Slackware Project. This event wouldn't be made.

    The event offers three categories of sponsorships, including:

    • Gold: Initial logo of 500x200 pixels.
    • Silver: Homepage logo of 300x250 pixels.
    • Bronze: Homepage logo of 170x150 pixels.

    Regardless of category the sponsor logo, will be distributed in all gifts given by the event ads and slides.

    Acess this link for prizes (In progress)

  • Donating to the event

    You can join our effort by donating any quantity for the event.
    Help will be the same finality of the listage we did above of the sponsors.

    Your name, if you wish, will be shown on the page of contributors.

    Acess our Crowdfunding (In Progress)

  • Helping organize the event in your city.

    A city will be chosen anually for the SLACKON happen. In case your city be chosen by the community, you can help organizing the event.

    If she's not the chosen one but you want to participate in the event where will occur you can help organize. In which way?

    • Organizing the event, Being part of the local team and going after a good localization, good hotels cheap meals, etc...
    • Lecturing
    • Creating workshops of a specific topic., for instance (Slackware Package), (How to collaborate on Slackware's wiki) etc...
    • Volunteering on the event to help organize the same on the days the SLACKON will happen.
    • Installing the system on Installfest.
    • Filming the event.
    • Photographing the event.

  • Other Ways

    There are other ways of you collaborating with the project and we need you in these more techincal areas. We use o github.com/slackcon as main platform for the developing.

    • Divulgation.
    • Art/Design.
    • Programming.
    • Correcting spelling errors, and increasing them turning more rich.
    • Ideas.
